Detailed Description

The class interfaces interest rate curves.

A interest rate curve is a representation of the relationship of rates in the swap or bond markets and their maturity and is used for the valuations of bonds or swap-related instruments. It is usually constructed with benchmark instruments and certain related contracts: market instruments that represent the most liquid and dominant instruments for their respective time horizons. More information see [16] [40] [20] [10] [6] .
